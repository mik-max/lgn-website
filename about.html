About Us

It is a network that will offer value to its individual members by exposing them to carry out entrepreneurial opportunities within their professional work fields across the globe.

Our Mission

To help integrate the Campus Ministry graduates into the church ministry through various transition programs and job specific trainings to prepare and set these graduates for a more brighter future.

Meet our Director

Passionate, Vibrant & Positively-Contagious are a few words to describe Pastor Doyin-Dennis Oshideko, PDD, as he is fondly called. PDD ventured into people development very early in life; from the age of 15, and for almost three decades, he has been actively involved in making lives better. A dynamic minister of the gospel, particularly dedicated to youths. He has ministered to youths in different parts of Nigeria and beyond.

PDD is also a Personal Performance Coach, Song Writer, TV Producer, TV Host, Actor, member of a Music Band, Audio Engineer & a Project Manager. These are the many colours of his beautiful coat. Thousands have been impacted by him; transforming, inspiring and healing many in the name of Jesus Christ. He is on a constant quest to build elite performers.
